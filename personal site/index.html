Unit I. The Nature of Science, Technology, and Mathematics 08 May 2024

In delving into the nature of science, I discovered that it is not merely a collection of facts or a static body of knowledge. Rather, science is a dynamic process—a continuous quest for understanding the natural world. Through systematic observation, experimentation, and the formulation of hypotheses, scientists unravel the mysteries of the universe. I learned that scientific theories are not absolute truths but rather our best explanations based on available evidence. This realization has fostered a sense of curiosity and humility, encouraging me to embrace uncertainty and appreciate the ever-evolving nature of scientific knowledge.
